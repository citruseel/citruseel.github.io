A multifunctional application to assist hospital staff and patients

In this project, we worked as a team of 11 to produce a multifunctional application for hospital staff and patients. Some functionalities include map viewing, editing, pathfinding, hospital service requests, and a chatbot for quick application navigation. To organize the project, we utilized Agile methodologies including daily scrum meetings and sprints. As the algorithms specialist, I was responsible for pathfinding which includes implementing breadth-first search, Dijkstra's, and A* using a euclidean-distance heuristic. However, I was also the Assistant Software Lead meaning I got familiar with and helped debug every aspect of the application. We successfully delievered the final application within an 8-week period.
