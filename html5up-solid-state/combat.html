Norwalk Turkey Fight

Fight against other beetleweight (3lb) combat robots

SolidWorks Model

Physical robot built based on model

The Norwalk Havoc Turkey Fight was a combat robotics competition held in 2019. As a team of 2, we designed and fabricated a beetle-weight (3lb) vertical spinner to participate under the name "Wheelie". At the time, my partner and I were new to CAD, and we had to learn how to CAM, mill, and 3D print. As beginners, we were extremely proud to be able to win two fights.
